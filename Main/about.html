Your Pizza Shop

Not just Another Baker

Pizza, Inc. is an American multinational pizza restaurant chain founded in 1960 and led by CEO Richard Allison. It works with IFTTT and has a iOS and Android app. The corporation is Delaware domiciled and headquartered at the Pizza Farms Office Park in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

As of 2018 Pizza had approximately 15,000 stores, with 5,649 in the U.S., 1,232 in India, and 1,094 in the U.K. Pizza has stores in over 83 countries and 5,701 cities worldwide.
